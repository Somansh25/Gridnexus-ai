Need Help?

Find answers here to help you learn how to play and have fun!

How does the AI opponent adapt to my playstyle?

Our AI uses a combination of reinforcement learning and pattern recognition to analyze your moves and adjust its strategy accordingly, providing a dynamic and challenging experience.

What are the differences between the game variants?

The Classic Nexus features a standard 3x3 grid, while the Expanded Matrix uses a 5x5 grid requiring 4 in a row to win. The Ultimate Nexus is a complex 9x9 grid with nested sub-grids, offering deep strategic gameplay.

How do I earn achievement badges?

Achievement badges are earned by reaching specific milestones, such as winning a certain number of games, achieving a winning streak, or completing challenges in different game variants. Check the Achievements section for details on how to unlock each badge.
